However , this is not given prime importance.

Preserving and managing natural habitats is essential for wildlife conservation. This includes protecting forests, wetlands, grasslands, and other ecosystems that provide shelter, food, and breeding grounds for wildlife. Species Preservation: Conservation efforts focus on safeguarding endangered and threatened species. This involves measures such as captive breeding, habitat restoration, anti-poaching efforts, and wildlife law enforcement. Sustainable Resource Use: Balancing human needs with conservation goals is important. Promoting sustainable practices for resource extraction, fishing, hunting, and tourism can help minimize the negative impact on wildlife and ecosystems.

Wildlife conservation refers to the protection, preservation, and management of wild animals, plants, and their habitats. It aims to ensure the long-term survival and well-being of various species and ecosystems. Wildlife conservation is crucial for maintaining biodiversity, promoting ecological balance, and preserving natural resources for future generations.
